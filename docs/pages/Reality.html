The reality is simple: with our greenhouses, every €1 million invested translates into 4,000 healthy meals served every single day.

This calculation is based on carefully optimized material costs: we guarantee the highest possible quality at the lowest sustainable price.

Each Missionsave greenhouse costs approximately €10,000 in materials. That means €1 million builds 100 greenhouses, and each greenhouse produces around 40 daily meals—adding up to 4,000 meals a day.

Maintenance and initial transport are not included in this base cost. However:

  • Transport is only needed once. Our greenhouses are designed with the same dimensions and fittings as shipping containers, making them easy and cost-effective to move worldwide. Transport costs vary by distance from our factory to the destination.
  • Maintenance is minimal. Optimized for efficiency, each greenhouse requires servicing only once every 5 years, with an estimated cost of no more than €1,000.

In short: Missionsave greenhouses turn one-time investments into long-lasting, scalable, and sustainable food security.
