MissionSave is reimagining how we design for sustainable robotics and agriculture. Our fully open‑source 3D design tool blends the simplicity of AutoCAD, the precision of FreeCAD, and the ease of Inventor — but in a lighter, faster, and fully parametric package.

From greenhouse frames to robotic components, MissionSave makes complex engineering accessible, accurate, and adaptable — for innovators everywhere.

MissionSave is a fully open-source initiative — explore the code and contribute here: 🔗 https://github.com/missionsave/missionsave.github.io

We’re building a next‑generation 3D design tool tailored for the MissionSave robot and greenhouse structure. Our goal: combine the simplicity of AutoCAD, the precision of FreeCAD, and the ease of use of Inventor — all in a lighter, faster, and highly parametric package.

Whether you’re designing structural frames, robotic components, or custom greenhouse layouts, our tool adapts to your needs with accuracy and flexibility.
